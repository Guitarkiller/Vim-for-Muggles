Change the first part of a line
===============================


Mission
-------

Replace the text preceding the dollar sign in each line with "The Dude".


Instructions
------------

  1. Search for the dollar sign `/@` and press Enter.
  2. Change the text to the start of the line with `c0`.
  3. Write "The Dude" and press Esc.
  4. Search again with `n` until you are in the next line.
  5. Repeat from step 2.
