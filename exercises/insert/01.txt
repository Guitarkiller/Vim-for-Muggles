Delete while inserting text
===========================


Mission
-------

Try the different ways of correcting mistakes while inserting.


Instructions
------------

  1. Insert a new line before the text using `o`.
  2. Write the same text "Butterfly ...".
  3. While writing correct your mistakes using: Ctrl-h, Ctrl-w and Ctrl-u


Text
----

Butterfly in the sky, I can go twice as high.
