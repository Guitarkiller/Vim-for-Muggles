Complete words while writing
============================


Mission
-------

Write the following words one per line: weird, accommodate,
handkerchief, indict, rhythm.


Instructions
------------

  1. Place the cursor in the bullet point.
  2. Insert at the end with `A`.
  3. Writing the first character of the word.
  4. Press Ctrl-p to see available completions.
  5. Keep pressing Ctrl-p/Ctrl-n until you are over the desired word and
     press Esc.
