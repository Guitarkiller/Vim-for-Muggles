Copy above the cursor
=====================


Mission
-------

Complete all the possible directions: north, east, west, south.


Instructions
------------

  1. Place the cursor in the last line and insert a new one with `o`.
  2. While in insert mode press Ctrl-y until the last word.
  3. Continue writing "east."
  4. Repeat until you complete all possible directions.


Text
----

- You can go: north.
